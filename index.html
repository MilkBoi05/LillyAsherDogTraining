A little about me

I'm based in Launceston, and I’ve completed a certificate in dog behaviour and psychology, as well as training to run puppy school. I’ve recently started working professionally as a trainer, but I’ve been learning and working with dogs for years.

My goal is to help dogs and their people build trust and understanding without using force or fear. I don’t follow a strict formula. Every dog is different, so my approach is flexible, gentle, and focused on what actually works for you and your dog.

Whether you’re dealing with a specific behaviour issue or starting out with a new puppy, I’m here to make things feel clearer and less overwhelming. I also have two dogs of my own, Obi (Labrador) and Posy (Mastiff), which keeps my approach practical and grounded in everyday life.

What I can help with

Puppy training

Foundations, toilet training, socialisation, gentle habits.

Obedience & manners

Sit, stay, recall, calm greetings, better focus.

Behaviour consults

Barking, reactivity, anxiety, over-arousal, problem-solving.

Loose-leash walking

Less pulling, more calm, enjoyable walks.

Recall training

Come when called, even with distractions.

Separation support

Gradual plans for home-alone confidence.
