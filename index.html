A little about me

I'm based in Launceston, and I've completed a certificate in dog behaviour and psychology, as well as training to run puppy school. I've recently started working professionally as a trainer, but I've been learning and working with dogs for years.

My goal is to help dogs and their people build trust and understanding without using force or fear. Every dog is different, so my approach is flexible, gentle, and focused on what actually works for you and your dog.

Whether you're dealing with a specific behaviour issue or just starting out with a new puppy, I'm here to help make things feel a bit more clear and a bit less overwhelming. If you're not sure where to start, I'm always happy to chat.

I also have two dogs of my own — Obi, a two-year-old Labrador, and Posy, a young Mastiff. Living with them has given me plenty of day-to-day experience working through different stages, temperaments, and challenges, which continues to shape how I work with clients and their dogs.
